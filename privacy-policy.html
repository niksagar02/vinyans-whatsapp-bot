Vinyans Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Vinyans respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you communicate with Vinyans, including through our website and WhatsApp services.

1. Information We May Collect

When you contact Vinyans, we may receive information such as your name, phone number, WhatsApp profile information, messages, educational interests, application requirements, and any information or documents that you choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to respond to enquiries, provide information about admissions, scholarships, mentoring, test preparation, and related services, improve our services, maintain communication records, and provide customer support.

3. WhatsApp Communication

When you communicate with Vinyans through WhatsApp, your messages may be processed using the WhatsApp Business Platform provided by Meta. Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to WhatsApp's and Meta's applicable privacy policies and terms.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with authorised Vinyans team members, mentors, service providers, or technology platforms when reasonably necessary to provide our services, operate our systems, comply with the law, or protect legitimate rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, to provide requested services, maintain appropriate records, or meet legal and regulatory requirements.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. However, no online communication or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may ask us to correct or delete personal information that you have provided, subject to legal, contractual, and operational requirements. You may also stop communicating with the Vinyans WhatsApp service at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Where a user is under the age legally permitted to provide consent, a parent or legal guardian should supervise the communication and provide any required consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact Vinyans through our official website: https://www.vinyans.com.
